Hello, I’m Nien-Cheng (Sam) Wu 吳念澄

A person who loves exploring the unknown and embracing challenges.

I’m a senior student majoring in Power Mechanical Engineering at National Tsing Hua University, currently interning with the Gaming ME Team at Logitech.

My research interests lie in control systems and robotics, and I’m also deeply passionate about sustainable energy and ICT education. Recently, I was honored with the Dr. Mei Yi-Chi Memorial Prize—an award presented to students who have made outstanding contributions to National Tsing Hua University. This recognition is not only a milestone but also a reminder of my aspiration to make a positive impact on the world through engineering.

If you share similar interests or values, feel free to explore my website or connect with me on LinkedIn.
